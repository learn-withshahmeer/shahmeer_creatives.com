Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, processes, and how we work with clients.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary greatly depending on the scope and complexity. A basic website might take 4-6 weeks, while a comprehensive branding and e-commerce solution could take 3-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation and project assessment.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, absolutely! We offer various post-launch support and maintenance packages to ensure your website and digital assets remain secure, updated, and performing optimally. This can include regular backups, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support.

How do you approach client collaboration?

Collaboration is at the heart of our process. We believe in transparent communication and involve our clients at every stage, from initial concept and strategy to design reviews and final deployment. We use collaborative tools to ensure a smooth and efficient workflow.

What is your pricing model?

Our pricing is project-based, tailored to the specific requirements and scope of each client's needs. We provide detailed proposals after understanding your objectives. For smaller tasks, we may offer hourly rates. We aim for transparency and value in all our pricing.
